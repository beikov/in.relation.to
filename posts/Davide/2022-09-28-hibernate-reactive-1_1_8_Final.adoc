:milestone: https://github.com/hibernate/hibernate-reactive/milestone/29?closed=1

https://hibernate.org/reactive/releases/1.1/#getting_started[Hibernate Reactive 1.1.8.Final] is now available!

This is a maintainace release that fixes a couple of bug fixes:

* https://github.com/hibernate/hibernate-reactive/issues/1386[ClassCastException when there is more than one lazy field on an entity]
* https://github.com/hibernate/hibernate-reactive/issues/1379[UnsupportedOperationException when saving entities in the database]
 
You can find {milestone}[the complete list of changes] on GitHub.

Thank you!

All details are available on the
